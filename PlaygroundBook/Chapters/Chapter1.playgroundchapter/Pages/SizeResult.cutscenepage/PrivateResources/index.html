Larger Size = Slower


In physics, mass = volume * density. In this case, increasing the size of a marble means there is more mass to move when rolling. This lowers the speed. (In real life it also means that a large marble's sustained speed is higher than a small marble. Our marble run has too many twists and turns for that to make a difference.)

We can simulate volume in SpriteKit using the initializer of SKPhysicsBody:

SKPhysicsBody(circleOfRadius: CGFloat)

Mass and Density can also be configured on an existing SKPhysicsBody:

SKPhysicsBody.mass = CGFloat
SKPhysicsBody.density = CGFloat
